Full Stack Developer

A Full Stack Developer is someone who works with the Back End — or server side — of the application as well as the Front End, or client side. Full Stack Developers have to have some skills in a wide variety of coding niches, from databases to graphic design and UI/UX management in order to do their job well. They are something of a swing, ready to assist wherever needed in the process. Needless to say, the salary for a Full Stack Developer is quite comfortable and can even be lucrative. Reports says that on average Full Stack Developer makes about PHP 600,000 per year.
