What is Computer Engineering

Innovative minds at work

Computer Engineering is concerned with the design and construction of computers and computer-based systems

Computer Engineering is a four-year engineering program that embodies the science and technology of design, development, implementation, maintenance and integration of software and hardware components in modern computing systems and computer-controlled equipment.

Turning ideas into reality

A computer engineer fuses electrical engineering and computer science to develop new technology

It focuses on the theories, principles, and practices of traditional electrical engineering and mathematics, and applies them to the problems of designing computers and computer-based devices.

Creativity is what we speak

Computer engineers evaluate, design, and maintain computer hardware and software systems

They develop, test, and design, computer processors, circuit boards, and network systems. They resolve problems or address any concerns that occur with the hardware or software and aid in the advancement of computer technology.

We Prioritize Growth

Computer engineers have a hand in creating robotics, networks, and other computer-based systems

They develop embedded systems, help design 3D printers, robots and many other digital devices, and they also study project management, testing, process control, mathematics and other topics.
